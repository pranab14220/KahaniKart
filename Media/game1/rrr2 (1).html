🌙 Raat Ki Sawari
Scene 1/6
Narrator Raat ke 8 baje the. Nisha office se ghar laut rahi thi. Bazaar ki galiyan dheere dheere khaali ho rahi thi.

Nisha "Aaj bahut thak gayi hoon... bas jaldi ghar pahunch jaun."
Narrator Achanak ek motorcycle uske paas aakar rukti hai. Rider use dekhkar halka sa muskuraata hai.

Biker "Excuse me... kya tum yahin aas-paas rehti ho?"

Nisha "Haan... lekin aap kaun ho?"
Narrator Us aadmi ki aankhon mein kuch ajeeb tha. Nisha ko ek pal ke liye darr mehsoos hua.

Nisha "Pata nahi kyun... mujhe thoda ajeeb lag raha hai."

Biker "Darne ki zarurat nahi hai. Mera naam Arjun hai."
Narrator Phir bhi pata nahi kyun, Nisha uski baaton par muskura deti hai.

Biker "Waise tumhari smile bahut khoobsurat hai."

Nisha "Haha... pehli baar mile ho aur itni tareef?"
Narrator Kuch hi der baad dono bike par baithkar shehar ki roshniyon se door nikal padte hain.

Biker "Tayyar ho? Ek yaadgaar ride ke liye?"

Nisha "Chalo... dekhte hain ye raat humein kahan le jaati hai."
🌙 THE END 🌙
